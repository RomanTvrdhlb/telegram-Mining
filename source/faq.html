How to mine?

For newbies

    • To make a deposit, please Log into your account.

    • Go to the "Balance" section, make a choice of currency and click on the "Deposit" button.

    • You will be redirected to a page that will give you a one-time wallet address to which you need to send a deposit. Deposit crediting usually takes a little time, but in some cases there is a delay of up to 12 hours.
